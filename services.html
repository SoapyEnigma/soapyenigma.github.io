Our Services

Professional custom apparel solutions for every need

Screen Printing

Our most popular service, screen printing delivers vibrant, long-lasting prints that stand up to countless washes. Perfect for bulk orders where consistency and durability matter.

  • Ideal for orders of 12 or more
  • Up to 8 colors per design
  • Works on cotton, polyester, and blends
  • Front, back, and sleeve printing available
  • Special effects: metallic, glow-in-dark, puff print

Heat Transfer & DTG

For full-color designs, photographs, or smaller quantities, our heat transfer and direct-to-garment (DTG) printing options deliver stunning detail and unlimited colors.

  • No color limitations
  • Perfect for photo-realistic designs
  • Great for small orders (even single items)
  • Soft hand feel on the garment
  • Quick turnaround available

Embroidery

Add a premium, professional touch to polos, caps, jackets, and more with custom embroidery. Perfect for corporate branding and uniforms that need to look polished.

  • Professional, upscale appearance
  • Extremely durable
  • Perfect for logos and text
  • Works on hats, polos, jackets, bags
  • Left chest, back, and sleeve placement

Design Services

Don't have artwork ready? No problem! Our design team can help bring your vision to life or create something completely new based on your ideas.

  • Logo creation and refinement
  • Converting images to print-ready files
  • Custom illustration and typography
  • Mockups before production
  • Design consultation included with orders
