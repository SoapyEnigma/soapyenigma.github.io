About Us

The story behind Show Ur Tees

Our Story

Show Ur Tees was founded with a simple mission: to help people express themselves through quality custom apparel. What started as a passion for creating unique designs has grown into a full-service custom printing business serving teams, businesses, and individuals throughout the community.

We believe that every project tells a story. Whether it's a sports team celebrating a championship season, a business building brand recognition, or a family commemorating a special reunion, we take pride in being part of those moments.

Our commitment to quality means we never cut corners. From the garments we source to the inks we use, every detail matters. When you wear a shirt from Show Ur Tees, you're wearing something made with care and craftsmanship.

What Sets Us Apart

Quality First

We use premium materials and proven techniques to ensure every print looks great and lasts. No cheap shortcuts, no compromises.

Personal Service

You're not just a number here. We work directly with you throughout the process, ensuring your vision comes to life exactly as you imagined.

Reliable Turnaround

We understand deadlines matter. When we commit to a delivery date, we deliver. Our efficient process means you get your order on time, every time.

Fair Pricing

Quality doesn't have to break the bank. We offer competitive pricing with transparent quotes—no hidden fees or surprise charges.

Experience

With years of printing experience and hundreds of satisfied customers, we have the expertise to handle projects of any size and complexity.

Local Business

We're proud to be part of our community. When you work with us, you're supporting local and getting the attention to detail that only a local business can provide.
